About Us

Welcome to Amore Coffee, a cozy haven nestled near the UITM campus in Samarahan, established in 2023. We take pride in serving high-quality coffee and creating a warm, inviting atmosphere for our customers. At Amore Coffee, we believe in the perfect blend of tradition and innovation. While we cherish the charm of the classic coffee shop experience, we're excited to embrace modern conveniences to enhance your visit.

Our upcoming Click & Sip Ordering System is designed to streamline your experience, allowing you to place orders online with ease. This system will reduce waiting times and ensure that your favorite coffee is just a few clicks away. Whether you're here to study, meet friends, or simply enjoy a moment of tranquility, Amore Coffee is committed to making each visit a delightful experience.
